Interesting Findings

These are some interesting things I found from the application:

  • The most common type of incident appears to be proactive police visits.
  • Between violent crimes and property crimes, property crimes are more common.
  • Most crimes seem to take place in Capitol River.
  • Out of violent crimes, aggrivated assault seems to be the most common incident.
  • Out of property crimes, auto theft seems to be the most common type of incident.
  • When looking just at the area around St. Thomas, proactive police visits and types of auto theft are the most common incidents.
